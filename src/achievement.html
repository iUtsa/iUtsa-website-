Project: Edith

Next Generation AI Calender: Edith is an intelligent calendar that not only plans your day but also considers your health, stress levels, and preferences. Through integration with multiple APIs, it suggests Spotify playlists for stress, plans academic tasks, offers horoscope-based guidance, allows conversational planning, and even suggests activities based on the weather. It's a complete, holistic approach to daily planning.

Technologies : HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Firebase, ExpressJS, NodeJS, React, Typescript, Git

Project: Hashimukh

HashiMukh is a comprehensive mental health assistant designed to support individuals through meaningful conversations and facilitate access to professional care. Powered by GPT API, HashiMukh engages users in empathetic and insightful chats, offering a safe space for mental wellness discussions. It also provides an easy and secure way to schedule highly privacy-protected appointments with psychiatrists, ensuring confidentiality and trust. With additional features tailored to assist users in managing their mental health, HashiMukh aims to be a reliable companion for emotional well-being.

Technologies : HTML5,CSS3,JavaScript,NodeJS,MongoDB
